Security Sector Reform
Horn of Africa · Regional Intergovernmental Organisation

SSR Workshop Design & Delivery

A regional intergovernmental organisation required Security Sector Reform training for a fragile state emerging from protracted civil conflict. Over a three-week sprint, BACS designed and delivered an immersive, syndicate-based workshop that became the model adopted for all remaining member-state SSR programmes.

The Challenge

Competing priorities between the sponsoring body and the fragile state; ambiguous requirements; wide disparity in practitioner skills; and funding constraints against high recipient expectations.

25+ SSR practitioners trained across defence and security institutions.
National Security Strategy roadmap developed and agreed.
SSR curriculum created, professionalising the discipline to attract talent.
Adopted as the model workshop for all remaining member-state SSR training.

SALW Governance
Horn of Africa · Regional Intergovernmental Organisation

SALW Governance & Capacity Building

An intergovernmental body required specialist capacity building to combat the illicit proliferation of Small Arms and Light Weapons in a fragile state context. BACS designed and delivered an immersive regional workshop, laying the foundation for a national Weapons and Ammunition Management policy.

The Challenge

Extremely low SALW practitioner skill levels; limited competency in policy development and data exploitation; and the complex interplay of actors driving illicit SALW circulation across the region.

25+ national SALW experts trained in evidence-based decision-making.
Effective measures agreed to strengthen institutional capacities.
National action plan produced to Detect, Deter and Disrupt illicit SALW circulation.
Foundation laid for a national Weapons and Ammunition Management Policy.

Experts Trained

Transport Management
South West England · Minibus Operator

Operator Licence Rescue & Compliance Transformation

A major minibus operator with a fleet of 80+ vehicles faced imminent loss of their operator licence following a Traffic Commissioner investigation. BACS provided urgent representation at the Public Inquiry then led a full compliance transformation, saving the business and its livelihoods.

The Challenge

Public Inquiry scheduled within four weeks of appointment; lack of client buy-in; conflicting shareholder priorities in a family-owned business; and deeply entrenched non-compliant behaviours across the fleet.

Successfully represented the company at the Traffic Commissioner Public Inquiry.
Operator licence retained. Traffic Commissioner paused withdrawal on the strength of a three-month roadmap.
15+ policies, procedures and guidelines produced and implemented.
80+ drivers trained. Paper records fully digitised to an AI-enabled cloud system.

Our Approach

The 4A Framework

A structured, collaborative methodology honed through real-world delivery across sectors that ensures clarity, accountability and results.

Assess

We conduct a deep-dive examination of your challenges and issues to map the 'As Is' state, understand underlying challenges and priorities, and define a clear baseline of requirements before any recommendation is made.

Architect

We co-design a tailored blueprint of the desired future state 'To Be', drawing on our multidisciplinary expertise to create a robust solution — implementable, sustainable and built around your specific context.

Activate

We mobilise rapidly and execute with precision — bringing the right people, technology and processes to bear. We maintain clear milestones, transparent reporting and senior accountability throughout delivery.
